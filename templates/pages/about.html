Just Eat It is my capstone project developed as part of The Data Incubator Fellowship Fall 2017 cohort in San Francisco.

Motivation

While travelling, here or overseas, food provides a window into other cultures and peoples. The simple act of sharing a meal can assist understanding and acceptance. However, if like my son you have a complex food allergy, fully participating in the travelling experience and finding suitable foods can be difficult and sometimes life-threatening! Food allergies have the following characteristics:
  • Effect many people in many ways
  • Are increasingly prevalent
  • Can have a serious health and social impacts
  • Are socially isolating
  • Are a significant remedial cost to society
Thus I have developed the "Just Eat It" app which allows users to register** and specify their allergy profile, combined with recipe data from Yummly to produce TripAdvisor-like personalised cuisine recommendations. ** An added benefit of the app is to create a crowd-sourced allergy database for research purposes.
